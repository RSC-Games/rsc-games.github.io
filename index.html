Welcome to RSC Games!

Who exactly is RSC Games?

We haven't properly introduced ourselves. We are RSC Games. We are an independent game developer that stands by the power of the indie game. We believe that the quality of a game doesn't necessarily come from the size of the team or the budget.

What, then, affects a game? The availability of good tools and, most important of all, passion. We are passionate developers who strive to provide excellent quality tools and applications, whether it is a game console, game engine, or superb game. We also believe strongly in supporting the homebrew community, from which many excellent games have been released.

Below, you'll find the products of our vision that have already become reality. Join us as we change the console and indie game development market for the better!
